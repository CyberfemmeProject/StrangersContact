Introduction: Strangers

Welcome to the game! A Chat Simulator exploring the theme of Cyberworld and online harassment.

In this game, you will take on the role of a young woman navigating the internet world. As the game progresses, you come across a chat program that allows you to talk to strangers online.

Please be aware that some content in this game may be triggering for some players.

If you are interested, please try Start Game or Share your story.

Good luck.
